# After After World — Quest Log & Narrative Synthesis

> *"The gods built a wall of crystal to keep the dark out. The crystals are cracking. Someone needs to hold the line — or find a way to sing louder than the thing on the other side."*

---

## High-Level Plot Structure

### Prologue — Before the Clock Started
The party washes ashore from Gamble Island after a tidal wave, kills cultists and a demon in **Saki**, and crosses paths with **Baz Stricknaz** — bastard prince of Jun, Reaper host, and would-be recruiter. After a brief alliance, the party turns on Baz; **Bram** kills him, triggering the **Reaper curse** — Baz becomes a ghost/reaper hybrid hunting Bram relentlessly.

The party travels to **Wip**, finds the city under siege by Newport forces (igloids, Melts family commanders), navigates the **Thieves Guild** (Dryzzlazz, Luna Cantine, Aegor), makes a deal with the Rakshasa **Caspian**, rescues **Luna Cantine**, and infiltrates **The Rock**. The party goes to Mont Kiara and **Asfache** destroys Caspian. **Ralf** is killed by Etheniss's sabotage in the Soul of the Rock but is resurrected via an igloid body, Ley Crystal shards, and his hat. **Catroniel** (revealed as an imposter) kills **Emma Wip** in a coup. **R4** impersonates Emma and takes control of Wip's government.

The party teleports to **Jun** for the Battle of the Ziggurat. **Heart Melts** (clockwork dragon) destroys the **Jun Ley Crystal**, unleashing aberrant creatures. Only the **Wip Ley Crystal** remains — roughly 5 years of power before Ghroth's path opens. The doomsday clock starts ticking.

### Act I — The First Run (Completed)
**The 5-Year Prep.** Gods appear in dreams, assigning divine missions. Alliances forged, La Bombas crafted, divine followers gathered, shapeshifters rooted out. The White Lion's writ obtained to counter the Siren Song. Meiko recruited to search for dragons. Cured Dryzzlazz, tried to broker Drow diplomacy. But in **Year 3 Fall**, Rat brings the Amulet of Null from Tiamat's tomb to the mortal plane — **Null the Dragon God** is freed and goes on a rampage, killing gods and destabilizing the divine forces at the Edge of Existence.

**The Final Battle.** The party destroys the last Ley Crystal to force Ghroth's arrival early. The Siren Song begins — its power causes mutations across the army before Steven silences it with the writ. Three Lieutenants appear. The battle goes poorly. The party uses the **Time Travel Scroll** to reset to the beginning of the 5-Year Prep.

### Act II — Farylse & The Chronologians
The party is intercepted by **Chronologians** (time cops). They fight through a stronghold with **Mordenkainen**, rescue **Freya**, and strike a deal with **Damian Heath**: he releases them to defeat Ghroth; if they succeed, he recruits them to upset the **Council of Eternity's** balance of power. The party explores **Farylse** — a shattered temporal plane where time is terrain — encountering the Unnumbered, Void, Weavers, Captain Jhodos, a Giant Robot, and a crazed long haired woman wearing a crown. They learn to control temporal terrain and gain decades of manipulable time. The party can never return to Farylse.

### Act III — The Second Run (Active Campaign)
**New Game Plus.** The party plane-shifts back to Wip at the start of the 5-Year Prep with full foreknowledge. Different choices are made — shapeshifters exposed immediately via Boop of Truth, Carxi sent to preemptively handle the Amulet of Null, alliances formed faster, Steven founds LIMA after assassinating the shapeshifter mayor of New Dwarf City.

**Year 1 Redux** sees the party split for optimized missions: Asfache unites the fire clans, Una finds Meiko and her dragon father Oman, Smolder infiltrates the Drow Underdark, R4 makes alliances in the mortal realm and beyond, Rat secures HIS Tower and sends Carxi to raid Tiamat's Horde, Sofia awakens travel trees toward an Ent Army. 

**Year 2 Redux** pushes into the **Mystica campaign** — Alexandria, Tyre, Lutetia — as R4 leads forces against shadow creatures plaguing the western continent. Steven surrenders the Everscale to a dying Essyliss. The Amulet of Plane Shift fails en route to the **Dragonsmoot**, stranding the party in **Sylvania**. It is Year 2 Winter.

## Quest Log

**Location:**  The Edge of Existence

**Status:** Active (endgame)

**Objective:** Gather forces and silence the Siren Song at the Edge of Existence — the incomprehensible frontline where gods battle Ghroth's forces.

If/when they can make Ghroth retreat, use the Primordial Shadows to follow him to the Far Realm. Any allied mortal forces will pursue Ghroth into the Far Realm, and end him for good.
